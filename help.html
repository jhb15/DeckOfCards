Instructions For Game

In this game there are 4 possible actions each denoted with a button at the bottom of the green area displayed.

Draw Cards Button

This button gives you the option to draw n cards from the deck, it will do this by displaying a prompts asking for a number.

Shuffle Deck

This button gives you the ability to shiffle the cards in the deck.

Order Hand

This button gives you the ability to order the cards currently in your hand.

Reset

This button gives you the ability to completely reset the game.
